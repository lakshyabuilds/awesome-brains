# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want this to be a place where anyone — developers, writers, researchers, students, and curious people — can contribute honestly without fear of ridicule or gatekeeping.

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**This community values:**

- Honest, specific feedback over vague praise or vague criticism
- Direct communication without being cruel
- Changing your mind when the evidence changes
- Disclosing conflicts of interest (e.g., "I work at this company")
- Helping newcomers contribute their first entry

**This community will not tolerate:**

- Harassment of any kind, public or private
- Dismissing contributions because of who submitted them
- Paid promotion disguised as honest curation
- Coordinated downvoting or brigading of tools for competitive reasons
- Sharing anyone's private information without consent

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to [@lakshyabuilds](https://github.com/lakshyabuilds). All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct, and to ban contributors who repeatedly violate it.
